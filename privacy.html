Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your data.

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how ControllerGene, owned and operated by DOOGLEX TECHNOLOGIES LLP, collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our LTO tape backup services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Billing and payment information
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Delivery addresses for tape shipments

2.2 Technical Information

  • IP addresses and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage patterns and service interactions
  • File metadata (size, type, upload timestamps)

2.3 File Data

We temporarily store your uploaded files for the sole purpose of creating LTO tape backups. We do not access, read, or analyze the content of your files beyond technical verification processes.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery: To process your data uploads, create LTO tapes, and deliver them to your specified address.

3.2 Account Management: To create and manage your account, authenticate users, and provide customer support.

3.3 Payment Processing: To process payments, issue invoices, and maintain financial records as required by law.

3.4 Communication: To send service updates, order confirmations, and respond to your inquiries.

3.5 Security: To protect our services, prevent fraud, and ensure data integrity.

3.6 Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

4. Data Security

4.1 Encryption: All data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption during transmission and storage. Your files are encrypted before being written to LTO tapes.

4.2 Access Controls: We implement strict access controls ensuring only authorized personnel can access systems containing your data.

4.3 Physical Security: Our data centers and tape creation facilities use enterprise-grade physical security measures including biometric access, surveillance, and environmental controls.

4.4 Network Security: We use firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and regular security audits to protect our network infrastructure.

4.5 Employee Training: All staff are trained on data protection protocols and sign confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Uploaded Files: Your uploaded files are securely deleted from our systems within 30 days after successful tape delivery.

5.2 Account Information: Account and billing information is retained for 7 years as required by financial regulations.

5.3 System Logs: Technical logs are retained for 12 months for security and troubleshooting purposes.

5.4 Backup Verification: Verification data and checksums are retained for 2 years to assist with potential data recovery requests.

6. Information Sharing

6.1 No Sale of Data: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

6.2 Service Providers: We may share limited information with trusted service providers (payment processors, shipping companies) who assist in service delivery.

6.3 Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government request.

6.4 Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, customer data may be transferred as part of business assets.

7. Your Rights

7.1 Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.

7.2 Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

7.3 Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.

7.4 Portability: You can request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.

7.5 Objection: You can object to certain types of processing of your personal information.

8. Cookies and Tracking

8.1 Essential Cookies: We use cookies necessary for basic website functionality, authentication, and security.

8.2 Analytics: We may use analytics tools to understand how our website is used and improve our services.

8.3 No Third-Party Advertising: We do not use cookies for advertising or share data with advertising networks.

8.4 Cookie Control: You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily processed and stored within India. If we need to transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place including encryption, contractual protections, and compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
