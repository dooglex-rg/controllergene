Frequently Asked Questions

What if my data is less than 2TB?

You'll still be charged for the minimum 2TB order (₹5,000). This ensures cost-effective service delivery and covers the fixed costs of tape creation and processing.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. The ₹2,500 per TB includes upload, processing, tape creation, verification, packaging, and standard delivery within India. Additional services are clearly priced.

How is data size calculated?

Data size is calculated based on the actual file sizes uploaded to our platform. We use binary calculation (1024 bytes = 1KB) and provide real-time size tracking during upload.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wire transfers. Payment is required before tape processing begins.
