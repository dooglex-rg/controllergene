Cancellation & Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent service policies

Last updated: August 29, 2025

Our Commitment

DOOGLEX TECHNOLOGIES LLP believes in helping its customers as far as possible, and has therefore established a fair and liberal cancellation and refund policy for our ControllerGene LTO tape backup services.

Cancellation Policy

Immediate Cancellation

Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made immediately after placing the order and before our technical team begins processing your data upload.

Processing Limitations

Cancellation requests may not be entertained if the orders have been communicated to our processing team and they have initiated the tape creation process or if data processing has begun.

LTO Backup Service Terms

For our specialized LTO tape backup services, the following terms apply:

Data Upload Phase

Once data upload begins, cancellation is only possible before our verification and processing phase starts (typically within 2-4 hours of upload completion).

Processing Phase

Once tape creation and data writing has begun, cancellation and refunds are not possible due to the irreversible nature of the LTO tape writing process.

Refund Policy

Quality Guarantee

Refund or replacement will be provided if the customer can establish that the quality of tapes delivered is defective or if there are verifiable data integrity issues.

Reporting Timeline

Any issues with damaged, defective, or non-conforming tapes must be reported to our Customer Service team within 7 days of receipt of the products.

Refund Process & Timeline

1

Report Issue

Contact our customer service with details and evidence of the issue.

2

Investigation

Our technical team will investigate and verify the reported issue.

3

Resolution

Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.

Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.

Warranty & Technical Support

For complaints regarding LTO tapes that come with manufacturer warranties, please refer the issue directly to the tape manufacturer first.

Note: Our service warranty covers the data writing process, verification, and delivery. Hardware warranty for the physical LTO tapes is covered separately by the tape manufacturer.
